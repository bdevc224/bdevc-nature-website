NATURE IS BEAUTIFUL

Learn fascinating things about nature and how we interact with it, both humans and animals alike.

Did You Know

Tree Communication

Trees communicates using an underground fungal network, sometimes called the Wood Wide Web to share nutrients and send warnings about droughts or insect attacks.

Tree Population

There are over three (3) trillion trees on earth, which is about 12 times more than the estimated stars in the Milky Way.

Peacock's Life Span

In the wild Peacocks can live for 20 years, but in captivity, they can live up to 40 years.

More Facts

Energy of The Hurricane

A single mature hurricane can release the energy equivalent of 10,000 atomic bombs in its rainfall alone.

Three-Hearted Octopus

The octopus has three hearts, two pumps blood to the gills, while the third pumps it to the rest of its body.

Tree Life Spans

Trees don't die of old age, they can live for thousands of years. The oldest known non-clonal tree is a bristlecone pine over 5,000 years old located at California's Inyo National Forest.
